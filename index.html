the unified life hub

A local-first personal workspace for notes, tasks, and calendar events. Your data stays on your device.

What it does

  • Captures notes, tasks, and events in one place.
  • Syncs two-ways with Google Calendar (events) and Google Tasks (tasks) when you connect them.
  • Runs entirely on your device. No account, no backend, no cloud copy of your data.

How sync works

When you connect a Google account, the app talks directly from your device to Google. Your notes, tasks, and events are stored locally, encrypted at rest with your password. Nothing is sent to the app's developer.
